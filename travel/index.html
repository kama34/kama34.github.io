The administrative center of Saitama Prefecture. The city is part of the metropolitan area of ​​Greater Tokyo. The area of ​​the city is 217.49 km² [1], the population is 1 250 928 people (August 1, 2014) [2], the population density is 5751.66 people / km². The city was formed on May 1, 2001 as a result of the unification of the cities of Urawa, Omiya [en] and Yono [en]. On April 1, 2005, the city of Iwatsuki was annexed to Saitama in the east. Saitama is a city designated by government decree from April 1, 2003.
